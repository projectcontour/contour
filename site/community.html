Do you want to help build Contour?

If you’re a newcomer, check out the Good first issue and Help wanted labels in the Contour repository.

Join the Contour Office Hours, a place where you can ask questions like “how do I do this in Contour” and “why does Contour do this thing this way?”

Join our Kubernetes Slack channel and chat with over 500 other community members: #contour

Get updates on Twitter at @projectcontour

Join the Project Contour Users mailing list for Contour news, release notifications, invitations to the community meetings, to discuss issues, or to provide suggestions and feedback.

Join the Contour Community Meetings on Zoom:

  • For Australia time zones:
    • Every first and third Tuesday at 6:30 PM Eastern Time / 3:30 PM Pacific Time / Wednesday at 9:30 AM Australian Eastern Time
  • For Americas time zones:
    • Every second and fourth Tuesday at 1 PM Eastern Time / 10 AM Pacific Time
  • Join the mailing list to get an automated invitation to the meeting
